ABOUT

Welcome to Whistling Maniac, a space born out of a profound passion for art and a deep-rooted belief in its transformative power. Founded with a vision to create a haven for artistic expression, Allan Ng'ang'a, the curator and founder, embarked on the journey to establish a unique art gallery that transcends traditional boundaries. Art, in its myriad forms, possesses the extraordinary ability to evoke emotions, challenge perspectives, and bridge gaps between diverse communities. The inspiration behind Whistling Maniac lies in the desire to cultivate a space where these experiences can unfold, where artists can thrive, and where art enthusiasts can embark on a journey of discovery. Having witnessed the profound impact that art can have on individuals and communities, I felt compelled to curate a collection that reflects the richness and diversity of human creativity. Each brushstroke, sculpture, or photograph showcased at Whistling Maniac is carefully selected to tell a story, spark dialogue, and inspire those who engage with it. The gallery is more than a physical space; it's a celebration of the human spirit, a testament to the boundless possibilities of artistic expression. It is a platform that honors both emerging and established artists, fostering an environment that encourages dialogue and collaboration within the art community. Whistling Maniac is not just about showcasing art; it's about creating an immersive experience where visitors can connect with the soul of each piece. It's about providing artists with a platform to share their narratives and push the boundaries of creativity. As we open our doors to art enthusiasts, collectors, and curious minds, we invite you to join us in this journey of exploration, appreciation, and celebration of art. Whistling Maniac is more than an art gallery; it's a commitment to fostering a cultural space where art thrives and becomes a catalyst for positive change.

ART MANAGEMENT AND TRAINING PROGRAMME

Our Art Management and Training Programme is designed to cater to both emerging artists seeking to refine their skills and seasoned professionals looking to elevate their art management capabilities. The program encompasses a diverse range of modules that cover key aspects of artistic development, management, and entrepreneurship.
Upon completion of the Art Management and Training Programme, participants can expect to:
Develop a strong artistic portfolio.
Acquire practical skills in gallery management and curatorial practices.
Navigate the art market confidently, understanding marketing and branding strategies.
Establish a network of valuable contacts within the art community.
Engage in entrepreneurial endeavors with a solid understanding of business planning and fundraising.
